Marino Beach Colombo

Marino Beach Colombo is a 5 star hotel situated in the heart of the capital city of Sri Lanka. The Hotel is designed with urban elegance and is located in close proximity to prominent places of interest. It is an ideal base for either business travelers or tourists in all aspects. The Hotel boasts of 300 spacious & luxurious rooms with sea or partial sea view. All rooms are equipped with modern amenities, a private balcony and also access to free Wi-Fi. The Infinity Pool and the rooftop Garden are iconic, providing a panoramic view of the Indian Ocean and the City. The Hotel also features Restaurants, Bars, Fitness Center and a Spa exclusively to uplift your stay with us. Adjacent to the Hotel is Marino Mall, which is idyllic for shopping, featuring an array of branded shops with diverse products. It also includes a Food Emporium, VR Centre and a 9D Cinema. Conference rooms are available to cater to the needs of our corporate clients, whilst the Grand Ball Room has the capacity to host up to 900 guests in various settings and is ideal for weddings, parties or other social events. Hotel Marino Beach is focused in continuing its expansion of true Sri Lankan Hospitality, to enable clients to experience an unrivaled service.
